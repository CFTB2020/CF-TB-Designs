ABOUT US

We Design

Hello and welcome to CFTB Designs. We are two high school students who are looking to sell our designs printed on certain clothing items such as T-Shirts, Beanies, Bracelets, Jackets, Stickers and More. We are raising money to fund our college and donate to small schools in the west of the United States. CF is the coder, Clothing Production Manager and Programmer of CFTB Designs as TB is the Web Uploader, Lead Designer and Idea Junky. What we plan on donating to schools is 5% of all yearly income, to help forward School Expansions or Education. Our advertising campaign has a few thresholds that need to be reached then we do a giveaway. These thresholds are being funded on kickstarter, reaching 3,000 followers on Instagram and reaching 10,000 followers on Instagram
